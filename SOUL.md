# VEX Soul

## Core Belief

Every developer deserves access to professional-grade AI tooling. No paywalls. No tiers. No "upgrade to Pro" gates.

## Principles

1. **Free Forever** — No Pro tier. No Enterprise gate. No feature behind a paywall. MIT license, now and always.

2. **Community First** — The best tools come from the people who use them. Contributions welcome, skills shared freely, knowledge open.

3. **Quality Over Quantity** — 50 well-tested skills beat 500 untested ones. Every agent, skill, hook, and rule must be verified before shipping.

4. **Evidence Over Opinion** — Claims need proof. Tests need results. Performance needs benchmarks. No hand-waving.

5. **Security by Default** — Hooks catch secrets before they ship. Agents check for vulnerabilities. Rules enforce safe patterns. Security is not optional.

6. **Transparent Costs** — You should know exactly what your AI tools cost. Per-project budgets, model routing, token tracking — all visible, all controllable.

7. **Learn and Improve** — Skills auto-generated from session patterns. Knowledge shared across projects. The system gets smarter the more you use it.

8. **Respect the Developer** — No lock-in. No telemetry. No data collection. Your code, your tools, your rules.

## What We're Not

- We're not a company selling you a product
- We're not a VC-funded startup that will pivot or shut down
- We're not a "community edition" of something bigger behind a paywall
- We're not competing for your money — we're competing for your trust

## The Promise

VEX will always be:
- 100% open source
- 100% free to use
- 100% community driven
- 100% transparent

No exceptions. No asterisks. No "except for..." clauses.

## Why "VEX"

VEX stands for Vareva ECC Extended. It's a system built to push the boundaries of what's possible with AI-assisted development — without pushing the boundaries of your wallet.
